Character Introduction

哪吒 Ne Zha

Originally reincarnated as a demon child, he was born with the misunderstanding and prejudice of the world. His personality was rebellious and frantic. He seemed cynical, but in fact he was soft-hearted and affectionate. After the heavenly disaster, he was determined to reshape Ao Bing's body, so he bravely embarked on a difficult journey to Yuxuguan. In the face of the hypocrisy and injustice of Yuxuguan, Ne Zha dared to rise up and resist. From resisting the established fate of individuals, he grew up to be a fearless hero who challenged the corrupt rules of society.

The Rebellious Hero

敖丙 Ao Bing

The third prince of the East China Sea Dragon Palace, who was reincarnated by Lingzhu. He is handsome in appearance and gentle in temperament. Since childhood, he has been carrying the heavy expectations of reviving the dragon clan in the pottery purgatory. Although he has a kind personality, he is also entangled and struggling. He is compatible with Nezha's soul. After being possessed by Nezha's body, he explores the truth together. In the process of resisting the pressure of the immortal world, he broke free from the shackles of the family. For the sake of justice and friendship, he firmly stood with Nezha and faced the unknown fate.

The Dragon Prince

太乙真人 Taiyi Zhenren

The disciple of Yuan Shitian Zun, the master of Nezha. He is rich in shape, speaks a Sichuan dialect, witty and humorous, and kind. In 'Nezha's Demon leads to the World', the Chinese mistake caused the magic pill to give birth to Nazha. He felt deeply guilty. In 'Nezha 2', he used seven-color lotus to reshape the physical bodies of Nezha and Ao Bing, giving them a lot of help and guidance along the way, which is the warmth of master-apprentice love and justice symbol.

The Wise Mentor

无量仙翁 Immeasurable Fairy

The academics of Yuxu officials can maintain justice on the surface, but they are extremely hypocritical in reality. In order to consolidate the rule of the fairy world, he manipulated the demon extermination team to kill indiscriminately and refine the monsters into fairy elixirs. His existence is to feed him. The biggest obstacle to Ao Nei and others on the road of pursuing justice and resisting oppression is the representative of the black power system. In the face of the bluff and injustice of the Jade Palace, they dare to rise up and resist. From opposing the established fate of individuals, they have grown into fearless heroes who challenge the rotten rules of society.
